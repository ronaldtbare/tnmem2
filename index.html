Welcome to Tennessee Memories!

Tennessee Memories

Tennessee Memories is nestled on 54 scenic acres near the Rutherford / Wilson County Line.  Beautiful country side, rolling hills and creeks are the back drop of the breathtaking property.   The surrounding area is full of American history from Native American, Revolutionary War, Civil War and maneuvers during WWII. 

 

Tennessee Memories is just a short drive away from Cedars of Lebanon State Park;  Nashville, Tennessee, the home of country music and a hop & skip down the road from Arrington Vineyard.

Weddings

A Tennessee Memories wedding includes a Ceremony Specialist to make sure your wedding plans come together effortlessly. You’ll enjoy unlimited emails, phone calls, and meetings with your ceremony specialist to make sure your wedding vision is brought to life on your very special day.

The Venue includes:

  • Use of grounds for engagement photo session
  • Outdoor ceremony site of your choice
  • Sunset Lodge or Eagle's Nest in the event of inclement weather
  • Sunset Lodge for indoor reception
  • Green space for tent area, games and socializing
  • Easy accessibility for elderly and handicapped
  • Convenient free parking
  • Access to the premises the day before to set up rentals


Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the price for a wedding at Tennessee Memories? Pricing is based the packages and options you choose. See the package information below on pricing and what is included in each package or contact Susan Slinker at Slinker@tennesseememories.org or (615)339-5384.
  • Is there a backup plan in the event of inclement weather? Yes, we can convert the Sunset Lodge or Eagle's Nest to a ceremony site.
  • Is the Eagle's Nest or Sunset Lodge climate controlled? Roll up canopy sides and ceiling fans serve for ventilation and cooling. Propane heaters are also available for rent. The Sunset Lodge offers central heat and air.
  • How many guests can be accommodated? The package pricing is for up to 200 guests in the Eagles Nest (if 60" round tables are used for seating) and 100 guests in the Sunset Lodge (if 60" round tables are used for seating)
  • Are tables and chairs available? No, tables and chairs must be rented from an outside vendor.
  • Can outside vendors be brought in? Yes, if it abides by the policies set forth in the signed contract by Tennessee Memories.
  • What is the smoking policy? Smoking is permitted only in designated areas. There is no smoking in any of the buildings or ceremony sites.
  • What is the cancellation policy? The deposit is non-refundable for the protection of both the renter and the venue. There is a date-change policy that allows for one date change if needed.
  • Can anything be tossed at the bride/groom or burned as they depart? Yes, but the item used must be pre-approved by Tennessee Memories.
  • How do I schedule an appointment to tour the property? Please call Susan Slinker at 615-339- 5384, or email at Slinker@tennesseememories.org. We would love to arrange a tour of the facility and answer any questions you may have about Tennessee Memories.
